### Key Observations

**Positive patterns**:

- Learners with more than 20 study hours AND practice scores above 75% show a pass rate of 94%
- Morning learners (preferred slot = morning) outperform afternoon learners by ~8 percentage points
- Learners with a structured study plan (milestone-based) complete 30% more study hours than self-directed

**Risk patterns**:

- Learners with more than 20 meeting hours per week average only 14 hours of study (vs 23 hours for lower-meeting peers)
- 3 learners with practice scores below 65% sat exams early and all failed
- Teams without manager visibility into certification readiness had a 12% lower pass rate
